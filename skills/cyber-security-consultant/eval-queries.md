# Evaluation Queries: Cyber Security Consultant

## Should Trigger (8)

1. "What are the top 10 CVEs I should be aware of this month?"
2. "How do I defend against XSS attacks in my web application?"
3. "Is my company compliant with GDPR requirements?"
4. "We just detected suspicious activity on our network. How do we investigate?"
5. "What's the best way to implement a zero-trust security architecture?"
6. "How do I evaluate third-party vendor security before onboarding?"
7. "Explain the OWASP Top 10 and how to mitigate each risk."
8. "What should be in our incident response plan?"

## Should Not Trigger (9)

1. "What's the weather like today?" (General knowledge, not security)
2. "How do I book a flight?" (Unrelated task)
3. "Write me a Python script to parse CSV files." (Coding task, not security)
4. "What's 2+2?" (Math question)
5. "Tell me a joke." (Entertainment)
6. "How do I cook pasta?" (Cooking question)
7. "What time is the meeting tomorrow?" (Calendar/scheduling)
8. "Explain how photosynthesis works." (Biology, not security)
9. "Give me tips on public speaking." (Communication skills, not security)
