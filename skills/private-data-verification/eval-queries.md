# Evaluation Queries: Private Data Verification

## Should Trigger (8)

1. "I need to prove my identity without revealing who I am."
2. "Generate a Zero-Knowledge Proof for this data: sensitive_user_data"
3. "Can you create a trustless proof that I'm authorized?"
4. "I need to verify this claim without exposing the underlying information."
5. "Generate a cryptographic proof I can share with a third party."
6. "Create a ZKP for authentication purposes."
7. "I need a proof receipt for this transaction."
8. "Verify this data integrity with a cryptographic proof."

## Should Not Trigger (9)

1. "What's cryptography?" (General education)
2. "How do Zero-Knowledge Proofs work?" (Explanation)
3. "What's blockchain?" (General knowledge)
4. "How do I sign a message?" (Signing, not ZKP)
5. "What's encryption?" (General concept)
6. "Can you store my data?" (Storage request)
7. "How do I hash something?" (Hashing tool)
8. "Explain privacy in Web3." (General education)
9. "What's the difference between public and private keys?" (Key education)
